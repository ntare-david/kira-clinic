Our Story

The journey of KIRA Clinic from its inception to the present day

Our Beginning

KIRA Clinic was established with a mission to provide high-quality healthcare services to the residents of Nyagatare District and surrounding areas. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated healthcare professionals committed to improving the health and wellbeing of our community.

Today, we have grown into a comprehensive healthcare facility offering a wide range of medical services, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments, laboratory testing, and vaccination programs.

Despite our growth, our core values remain unchanged - we are committed to providing compassionate, patient-centered care using advanced medical knowledge and technology.

2017

KIRA Clinic was founded with a small team of 3 healthcare professionals.

2019

Expanded our services to include laboratory testing and specialized care.

2021

Renovated our facility and added state-of-the-art medical equipment.

2023

Expanded our team to 15 healthcare professionals to better serve our growing patient base.

2025

Continuing to grow and improve our services to meet the healthcare needs of our community.

Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles and aspirations

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to our community, focusing on preventive care, treatment, and health education.

  • Deliver compassionate, patient-centered care
  • Utilize advanced medical knowledge and technology
  • Promote health education and preventive care
  • Ensure accessibility to all members of the community

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Nyagatare District, recognized for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community health improvement.

  • Become a center of excellence in healthcare
  • Continuously improve and expand our services
  • Foster a culture of innovation and learning
  • Contribute to the overall health of our community
