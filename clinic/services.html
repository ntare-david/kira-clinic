Healthcare Services

At KIRA Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services to meet the needs of our community. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is committed to providing high-quality care using advanced medical knowledge and technology.

General Medical Consultations

Our experienced doctors provide comprehensive medical consultations for patients of all ages. Whether you're dealing with a common illness, chronic condition, or need preventive care, our team is here to help.

  • Routine check-ups
  • Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses
  • Management of chronic conditions
  • Preventive healthcare

Laboratory Testing

Our state-of-the-art laboratory offers a wide range of diagnostic tests to help identify and monitor health conditions. Our skilled technicians ensure accurate and timely results.

  • Blood tests
  • Urine analysis
  • Microbiology tests
  • Rapid diagnostic tests

Vaccination Services

We offer comprehensive vaccination services for children, adults, and travelers. Our vaccination programs follow international guidelines to ensure optimal protection against preventable diseases.

  • Routine childhood vaccinations
  • Adult immunizations
  • Travel vaccines
  • Seasonal flu shots

Specialized Care

In addition to general medical services, we offer specialized care in several areas to address specific health needs of our patients.

  • Maternal and child health
  • Non-communicable disease management
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Health education and counseling

Need Medical Assistance?

Our team is ready to help you with any health concerns.
